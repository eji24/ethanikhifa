Ethan Ikhifa — NYC / LA / EU

"I don't just shoot — I see. Every frame is a decision. Every edit is a world."

24, Brooklyn. Videographer, model, and creative director working across fashion, music, and film. I shoot independently — no crew, no production company. Just a camera, an eye trained by years in front of the lens, and a CS degree that makes me think in systems.

I've walked Off-White and Campillo. Made short films in Brooklyn at 2am. Built the Moon & Cross visualizer series from scratch. This is all one continuous body of work.
